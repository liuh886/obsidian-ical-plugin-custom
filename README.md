## 中文 - 连接 AI Agent 与现实世界的日历桥梁

**Agent 安排的工作如何既沉淀在 Obsidian Day Planner 中，又能被 Google Calendar 同步？iCal Pro！**

iCal Pro 是一款专为 AI 驱动工作流设计的同步引擎。它能将你在 Obsidian 笔记中记录的、或由 AI Agent 自动生成的任务，无缝桥接到 Google Calendar、Apple Calendar 等全球日历生态中。

### 🌟 为什么选择 iCal Pro？
- **🚀 极速增量索引 (O(1) Cache)**：针对大型知识库优化。不再全库扫描，仅在文件修改时瞬间更新，即使有上万篇笔记也丝滑流畅。
- **🌍 时区自适应**：彻底解决跨国出行、异地差旅时的 8 小时时差偏移问题。
- **📝 多行上下文抓取**：不仅仅是标题。它能聪明地抓取任务下方的路线详情、航班号或会议摘要，并同步到日历描述中。
- **🔗 一键回跳 Obsidian**：日历事件附带深度链接，点击即可从手机/电脑日历直接唤起对应的 Obsidian 笔记。

## English - The Calendar Bridge for AI-Driven Workflows

**How can tasks assigned by AI Agents stay in your Obsidian Day Planner and sync to Google Calendar simultaneously? iCal Pro!**

iCal Pro is a high-performance synchronization engine. It ensures that tasks generated by your AI agents or recorded in your daily plans are seamlessly bridged to your global calendar ecosystem (Google, Apple, Outlook).

### 🌟 Core Features
- **🚀 Incremental Indexing (O(1) Cache)**: Built for scale. Instead of scanning your entire vault every time, it only updates modified files. Instant sync for 10 or 10,000 notes.
- **🌍 Travel-Ready Timezones**: Systematic "Floating Time" support combined with `X-WR-TIMEZONE` headers. Your schedule stays accurate, whether you're in Singapore or Shanghai.
- **📝 Rich Context Capture**: Intelligently captures notes, quotes, and sub-items listed *under* your task and injects them into the calendar event description.
- **🔗 Deep Linking**: Every event includes a direct `obsidian://` link. Go from your calendar app back to your specific vault note in one click.

## ⚙️ Getting Started / 快速上手

1. **Setup GitHub Sync**: Create a [GitHub Token](https://github.com/settings/tokens?type=beta) (Gist scope) and a [Gist](https://gist.github.com/).
2. **Configure**: Enter your Token and Gist ID in Obsidian Settings -> **iCal Pro**.
3. **Subscribe**: Copy the generated **Subscription URL** and add it to Google/Apple Calendar.
